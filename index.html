History Present Plans

I began my programming journey, with Python. I was mainly solving some simple problems on a phone application. It was not so entertaining to me at moment, but it kept me busy between breaks in middle school.

Later in technical school, I learnt basics of C++ mixed with C. At first I did not like it, but with time I was favouring it more and more and soon C++ became my favorite programming language. With it, we did some introductions to algorythms and their concepts.

Other than that, I also got introduced to developing websited using HTML, CSS, JavaScript and later PHP with SQL. Learning all those stuff at the same time, was interesting and being able to create my own website, than adding some cool effects and finally making it more advanced by manipulating database was very satisfying.

Currently I am an applied computer science student at AGH University of Science and Technology and an intern at Leading Devices.

In my free time, I also program some simple programs while trying to learn new technologies.

In the Future I am planning on learning more about embedded systems, tools used to develop them and electronics with commonly used interfaces and their standards, to be able to create more advanced things.
